CrunchDigitalRPG is a system designed around automation for FoundryVTT.

All tabletop RPGS have been designed around the limitations of pen and paper.
But computers have been ubituitous for many years now; And tabletop roleplaying has become a popular online hobby.
Crunch is an effort to realize what can be done with tabletop RPGs if paper is ignored and computers become the focus.
Digitize every aspect of the game. Infinite resolution, granularity, and complexity, with no more stress on the player.

As an example, there would no longer need to be a static, or simple turn system. Thousands of variables could influence the moment at which a player takes their turn.
Many more thousands of variables could influence any aspect of a skill test or conflict.
Rolls no longer have to be restricted to the distrubtion of their dice.
More than just 5% increments of a d20, but hundreths of a % based on the variables influencing the roll.

It will be the purest form of a crunchy ttrpg without any of the pains of crunchy pen-and-paper ttrpg.
